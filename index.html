Welcome to Liza's Pokemon!

In this game, you'll be able to catch and collect all kinds of Pokemon. Use the arrow keys to move up, down, left, and right. The orange tiles are path tiles, where wild Pokemon won't attack, but they will on the green tiles! Walking on a pink tile will open the Shop, where you can save, buy Pokeballs to catch more amazing Pokemon, buy potions to heal your Pokemon in-battle, and heal your Pokemon for future battles.
